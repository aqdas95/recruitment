FAQ’s


Q1. How can I contact someone for help on the app?

You can call on - 01908 533240

App support email: appsupport@pertempsmedical.co.uk


Q2. How can I change my password?

From the side menu select . This will bring you to a screen where you enter your old password and can then confirm your new password.


Q3. How do I edit my profile?

Open the side menu by selecting the list symbol open up your profile to edit your profile select the pencil icon once you have finished editing. You can save your changes using the save button in the top right corner.


Preferences

Q4. How do I update my availability?

In the side bar select “preferences” choose the availability type you want to edit and update your availability once completed select save.


Q5. How can I amend the distance from my postcode, so I will receive vacancies for a further radius?

In your preferences, you can use the slider bar to select the number of miles you are willing to receive vacancies from your post code.


Q6. How do I switch off certain availability calendars?

You can turn your availability calendars on or off for each of your short term, long term or permanent calendars by selecting the blue and white slider button next to the calendar you would like to amend.


Compliance

Q7. If I am unable to upload my documents on my app can I submit these another way?

Yes, if you are unable to upload compliance documents via the app then you can email these to your compliance officer or alternatively complianceactions@pertempsmedical.co.uk and these will be processed and added to your preferences by your compliance officer.


Vacancy Feed

Q8. How do I view a Vacancy details?

In the side menu on vacancy feed, select the vacancy you want to view and all the vacancy details page will open up. You can also view any attached documents if there are any relating to the job here as well (EG: Job Description)


Q9. How do I apply for a job?

In the side menu on vacancy feed, select the job you want to apply for and select apply now, alternatively you can do this in the same way within the shortlist tab.


Placed

Q10. How do I view what vacancies I have been booked for?

On the side menu, you will have a placed tab. If you click in here, you will be able to view all of your past and previous bookings and relevant contracts.


Q11. What does shortlist mean?

From your vacancy feed, you can quickly look through and select vacancies to short list . These are jobs that you will then be able to view in your short list screen from the side menu when you are ready to review.


Q12. What happens if I block notifications from a hospital?

If you block notifications from hospitals this will show you no notifications for that hospital until you unblock the notifications.


Q13. Can I apply for a job I have already said I am not interested in on the app?

Yes you can you just need to open your side menu tab, select the not interested icon at the bottom of the screen and view the job. Once you view the job you can either move this into your shortlist or you can apply for it.


Q14. How can I find vacancies I have applied for?

On the side bar you will have a jobs applied section. Here you will see a list of vacancy numbers and details of the vacancies you have applied for.
